Pepperoni pizza

Pepperoni is a common topping for pizza made out of beef or pork. It is considered a type of sausage and can be mildly spicy. Pepperoni pizzas are cheese pizzas with pepperoni on top or underneath the cheese. It is the most common and popular pizza topping.
